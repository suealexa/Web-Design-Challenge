Summary: How latitude impacts weather

This project examined the effect of latitude on four different weather types. We utilized our knowledge of API calls to gather data from over 500 randomly selected cities around the world. We then used Matplotlib tools to create four visualizations that illustrated the relationship of cloudiness, temperature, humidity and wind speed to latitude.

Finally, we analyzed the results of climate on latitude. This site summarizes the findings and includes the source material and visualizations.
